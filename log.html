THE BIRTH OF EYEBROWZR

I've been making music for a long time. Half finished projects, songs that lived on hard drives and never saw the light of day. A lifelong passion for video games and a head full of game ideas that never quite made it off the drawing board. The usual story.

EYEBROWZR started as a way to stop overthinking and just ship things. Give it a name, give it a face, separate it from my ego enough that I could actually put it out into the world without second guessing every decision.

What started as an alias became something more — a brand, a persona, an aesthetic. A lens that everything passes through. The music sounds a certain way because of it. The games look a certain way because of it.

What you're looking at now is an artistic playground and a carefully constructed ecosystem — music, art, and games feeding into each other, one project fueling the next.

Stick around, the adventure is just getting started.

— EYEBROWZR
